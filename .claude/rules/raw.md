# Reading Phase Rules

Extract and present content from URLs. Do not write to disk.

## Extraction Format

```yaml
source: [Author/Handle]
title: [Original title or first 15 words]
date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
url: [Direct link]
category: [Metric|Best Practice|Tool/Feature|Concept|Case Study|Opinion/Critique|Tutorial|Trend]
```

## Content Structure

**Core Claim** — 1-2 sentence thesis  
**Key Points** — 3-5 bullets with data/examples  
**Critical Details** — Exact numbers, tools, timelines  
**Notes** (optional) — Connections, gaps, questions

## Standards
- 80-150 words (excluding metadata)
- Preserve exact numbers and quotes
- No editorializing — facts only

## Categories
- **Metric**: Data, benchmarks, measurements
- **Best Practice**: Actionable advice, methodology
- **Tool/Feature**: Specific product or capability
- **Concept**: Theory, framework, or model
- **Case Study**: Real-world implementation
- **Opinion/Critique**: Author's perspective
- **Tutorial**: Step-by-step instruction
- **Trend**: Market observation or forecast
